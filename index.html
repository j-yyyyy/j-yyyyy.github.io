About Me

I am an incoming Ph.D. student at the School of Intelligence Science and Technology, Nanjing University. I am also a research intern on Kuaishou's Kling team.

My research focuses on multimodal large language model reasoning, planning, evaluation, and efficient multimodal systems. I am very interested in research on multimodal agents, particularly topics related to embodied agents that align with embodied intelligence.

Research

  • Audio-visual and omni-modal understanding for multimodal large language models.
  • Efficient omni-modal modeling, token compression, and evaluation.
  • Multimodal reasoning and planning for Agent-based solutions to complex Real-world Problems.
